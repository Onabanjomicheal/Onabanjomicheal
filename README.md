## 👋 About Me

I'm **Onabanjo Michael Oluwatobi** — an **Electrical & Electronics Engineering** graduate from **Olabisi Onabanjo University, Lagos, Nigeria**, working at the intersection of **machine learning**, **transportation engineering**, and **logistics systems**.

My work is driven by a single frustration: movement is broken. Packages arrive late. Commuters sit in traffic for hours. Emergency vehicles crawl through gridlock. Supply chains fail at the last mile. These are not small inconveniences — they are systems problems, and I use data and AI to solve them.

I build end-to-end solutions that span the full mobility stack:

- 🚦 **Urban Traffic Systems** — using Deep Reinforcement Learning and SUMO microsimulation to optimize signal timing, reduce congestion, and cut emissions at intersections
- 🚚 **Logistics & Delivery Intelligence** — analyzing last-mile delivery failures, identifying delay patterns in supply chains, and building predictive models to improve fleet and route performance
- 🌉 **Infrastructure Research** — studying how Autonomous Vehicles change traffic dynamics on real African corridors like Third Mainland Bridge
- 🧠 **Production ML** — deploying end-to-end pipelines from Jupyter notebooks to AWS-hosted inference APIs, with MLOps best practices
- 📊 **Data-Driven Decision Making** — turning messy operational data (logistics networks, energy grids, insurance claims, ship clusters) into actionable insight

> *"Movement is the lifeblood of any economy. When it breaks down — whether a delivery that never arrives or a city brought to a standstill — people and businesses pay the price. I build systems that make movement smarter, faster, and fairer."*

Deep Q-Network agent optimizing traffic signal timing in SUMO, modeled on Lagos traffic. Reduces average waiting time and emissions at congested intersections.
`Python` `DQN` `SUMO` `Deep RL` `TraCI`

### 🌉 [Third Mainland Bridge AV/HDV Simulation](https://github.com/Onabanjomicheal/third-mainland-av-hdv-simulation)
Analyzing traffic, emissions, and platooning effects of AV penetration on Third Mainland Bridge — one of Africa's longest bridges.
`Python` `SUMO` `Autonomous Vehicles` `Platooning` `Lagos`
